# Refund and Subscription Retention Policy

## Standard Refund Windows
* We offer a strict 14-day money-back guarantee for all newly initiated subscriptions.
* No automatic refunds are processed after 14 days of account operation.

## Exceptions and Billing Adjustments
* Exceptions are granted if prolonged platform bugs or verifiable system lag caused a total interruption of business continuity for more than 3 consecutive days.
* Approved refunds will be processed back to the original payment method within 5-7 business days.

## Customer Churn Retention Playbook
* If an active user threatens to churn or post negative reviews due to unresolved issues or support dissatisfaction, support agents should offer a 1-month service credit or free temporary seat upgrade rather than processing an immediate subscription cancellation.
