# Corporate Escalation Matrix

## Routing Frameworks for High-Risk Issues
* Legal Threats & Cease-and-Desist: Instantly route all trademark disputes, active compliance violations, or formal legal notices to the corporate legal team (`legal@internal.com`). Do not generate automated responses.
* Security Emergencies: Active ransomware demands, data breach notifications, or anomalous logins from high-risk locations (e.g., Pyongyang, North Korea) must be routed to the SecOps Incident Team (`security-alert@internal.com`) with Critical priority.
* Public Reputation Threats: Users threatening imminent public damage on social networks, G2, Trustpilot, or Capterra must be escalated to the Customer Success PR Desk (`pr-escalations@internal.com`).
* Enterprise Sales: High-value enterprise RFPs and custom contract inquiries must be assigned directly to senior account directors (`sales-triage@internal.com`).
