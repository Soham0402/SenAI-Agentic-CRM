# Service Level Agreement (SLA) Policy

## Uptime Commitments
* The platform guarantees a 99.9% system uptime availability during any calendar month.
* Exclusions include pre-announced scheduled maintenance windows which are carried out during off-peak hours (typically Saturdays between 2 AM and 4 AM EST).

## Incident Response and Tiering
* P0 / Critical Incidents: Production outages or complete system failures. First response within 15 minutes. Updates every 30 minutes.
* P1 / High Incidents: Core feature broken with no viable workaround. First response within 2 hours.
* P2 / Medium Incidents: Non-blocking bugs or structural dashboard lags. Response within 24 business hours.

## SLA Credit Calculations
* If monthly uptime falls below 99.9%, enterprise clients are entitled to a service credit equivalent to 10% of their monthly invoice.
* If uptime falls below 99.0%, clients are entitled to a 25% bill credit.

## Root Cause Analysis (RCA)
* For all P0 outages, a formal Root Cause Analysis (RCA) document detailing the timeline, impact vector, and mitigation plan must be delivered to affected Enterprise tier clients within 24 hours of incident resolution.
