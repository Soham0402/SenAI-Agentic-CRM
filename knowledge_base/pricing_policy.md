# Pricing Policy and Tiers

## Subscription Tiers
* Starter Plan: $49/month. Includes 2 seats, basic dashboard, and 100 API requests/minute.
* Standard Plan: $199/month. Includes 10 seats, full analytics, and 1,000 API requests/minute.
* Pro Plan: $299/month. Includes 25 seats, advanced AI automation, and 2,500 API requests/minute.
* Enterprise Plan: Custom pricing starting at $1,200/month. Custom seat limits, dedicated support, and up to 10,000+ API requests/minute.

## Special Discounts
* Non-profit Discount: Registered 501(c)(3) organizations are entitled to a 30% discount on the Standard Plan. This reduction applies to the base cost and any additional seat purchases made for that plan tier.
* Educational Pricing: Universities and educational institutions can claim up to 50% off standard commercial rates for active classroom instructional tracks.

## Upgrades and Pro-rata Billing
* Upgrades made during a billing cycle are calculated on a pro-rata basis. The user's account is billed immediately for the net difference covering the remaining active days in the current month's cycle.
